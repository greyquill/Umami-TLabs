3. Growth Funnel Design

The growth funnel is designed to work in three interconnected stages, where each stage reinforces the others:

Awareness (SEO + Ads)

SEO Foundation: Build structured content around your lab's services, health check-up packages, and test capabilities. Focus on niche and locality-based keywords rather than competing head-on with large national labs. This creates sustainable, organic traffic over time.

Targeted Digital Marketing: Use Google Ads, Facebook Ads, Instagram, and YouTube to create targeted campaigns. The AI-driven ad ecosystem ensures your content reaches the right audience demographics. Ads and SEO work together—SEO drives organic discovery, while ads create immediate visibility and engagement.

Content Strategy: Create neutral, informative content that guides potential customers. This includes educational content about health packages, test types, and preventive care—positioning your lab as a trusted local healthcare partner.

Conversion (Packages + Booking)

Service Packaging: Structure your tests into clear health packages (Basic, Medium, Pro, Full) similar to larger healthcare providers. This simplifies decision-making for customers and creates clear value propositions.

Seamless Booking Experience: A web-based platform that allows customers to:

  • Browse available tests and packages
  • Book appointments online
  • Schedule sample collection (home collection or lab visit)
  • Complete transactions digitally

The platform will be designed to compete with or exceed the experience provided by platforms like Orange Health or Thyrocare, regardless of lab size.

Retention (Reminders + Repeat Tests)

Automated Engagement: Once a customer registers, the system will:

  • Send automated reminders for repeat tests based on medical recommendations
  • Provide real-time updates via SMS, WhatsApp, or Telegram
  • Notify customers about test progress and result availability
  • Create a continuous engagement loop that keeps your lab top-of-mind

Repeat Test Automation: Packages need not be one-time. The system can identify when a test should be repeated (e.g., quarterly health check-ups) and proactively reach out to customers, converting one-time transactions into recurring revenue streams.

6. Metrics & Tracking

We will track key metrics at each stage of the funnel to measure progress and optimize performance:

Awareness Metrics

  • Organic Traffic: Website visits from search engines
  • Search Rankings: Position for target keywords
  • Ad Impressions & Reach: Number of people seeing your ads
  • Click-Through Rate (CTR): Percentage of people clicking on ads or search results

Conversion Metrics

  • Inbound Inquiries: Number of people contacting the lab
  • Booking Rate: Percentage of inquiries converting to bookings
  • Cost Per Lead (CPL): Marketing spend divided by number of leads
  • Cost Per Booking: Total acquisition cost per completed booking

Retention Metrics

  • Repeat Customer Rate: Percentage of customers booking again
  • Customer Lifetime Value (CLV): Total revenue from a customer over time
  • Engagement Rate: Response to notifications and reminders

Operational Metrics

  • Capacity Utilization: Percentage of available time slots filled
  • Average Booking Lead Time: Time between inquiry and booking
  • Platform Usage Rate: Percentage of bookings through platform vs. manual
Reporting: We will provide regular reports (weekly during active phases, monthly during optimization) showing progress against targets, with recommendations for adjustments based on data.
