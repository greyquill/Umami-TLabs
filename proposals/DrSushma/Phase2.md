### **Phase 2: The Plan You’ll Share (What the Document Should Contain)**

Think of the document as **“Plan, not Proposal”**.

#### **Recommended Structure (8–10 pages max):**

1. **Executive Summary (1 page)**
   * What problem we’re solving
   * What success looks like in 90 days
2. **Current State vs Target State**
   * Today: fragmented, manual, low visibility
   * Target: discoverable, trackable, scalable
3. **Growth Funnel Design**
   * Awareness (SEO \+ Ads)
   * Conversion (Packages \+ Booking)
   * Retention (Reminders \+ Repeat tests)
4. **Phase-wise Execution**
   * Phase 1 (Weeks 1–4): SEO \+ Content \+ Landing
   * Phase 2 (Weeks 5–8): Ads \+ Funnel optimization
   * Phase 3 (Weeks 9–12): Platform workflows \+ engagement
5. **Platform Scope (Web-first)**
   * Booking
   * Sample pickup
   * Test lifecycle visibility
   * Notifications (SMS/WhatsApp)
6. **Metrics & Tracking**
   * Traffic
   * Leads
   * Bookings
   * Repeat customers
7. **Dependencies from Lab**
   * Tests list
   * Packages
   * Capacity
   * Operational SOPs
8. **Commercial Models (High-level, no numbers yet)**
   * Fixed \+ variable
   * Outcome-linked
   * Phased activation
