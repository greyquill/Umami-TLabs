Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) & Financial Analytics

Optimize practice solvency with an automated, end-to-end financial ecosystem designed to minimize claim rejections and accelerate days sales outstanding (DSO).

Technical Overview

Our RCM module integrates directly with national payer gateways to provide real-time eligibility verification and claim status tracking. By leveraging predictive analytics, the system identifies potential coding errors (CPT/ICD-10) before submission, reducing denial rates by an average of 35% within the first quarter of implementation.

Core Capabilities

  • Automated Claim Scrubbing: Pre-submission algorithms analyzing claims key-value pairs against payer-specific rulesets.
  • Denial Management Workflow: Categorized work queues for rejected claims with root-cause analysis tooltips.
  • Patient Financial Experience: Digital wallet integration (Apple Pay, Google Pay) and automated payment plan structuring.
  • Ledger Reconciliation: Daily automated sync (EOD) with bank deposits and ERP systems.

Implementation Timeline

Week 1: Gateway Configuration. Secure API handshake with clearinghouses and merchant processors.
Week 2: Historical Migrations. Transfer of aging AR (Accounts Receivable) and patient ledgers.
Week 3: Rule Definition. Customizing fee schedules and denial routing logic.
Week 4: Live Transactional Stream. Full cutover to automated billing cycle.

Electronic Medical Records (EMR/EHR)

A longitudinal, interoperable patient data repository designed for high-velocity retrieval and secure interoperability (FHIR/HL7).

Technical Overview

Our cloud-native architecture ensures sub-second query response times for patient histories, regardless of dataset size. Security protocols include AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit, fully compliant with HIPAA, GDPR, and SOC2 Type II standards.

Core Capabilities

  • Interoperability: Seamless HL7 data exchange with external labs (Quest, LabCorp) and hospital systems.
  • Role-Based Access Control (RBAC): Granular permissions ensuring staff strictly access "Need-to-Know" PHI.
  • Integrated Vitals Tracking: Direct IoT ingestion from connected blood pressure cuffs and scales.

Implementation Timeline

Week 1: Chart Digitization. Professional scanning team digitizes active paper charts.
Week 2: Cloud Migration. Encrypted transfer of digital files to HIPAA-compliant cloud storage.
Week 3: Data Verification. Providers verify data integrity before archiving physical records.
Week 4: Go Live. Full EMR system launch with staff training and support.

Family Practice, Seattle
