Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) & Financial Analytics

Optimize practice solvency with an automated, end-to-end financial ecosystem designed to minimize claim rejections and accelerate days sales outstanding (DSO).

Technical Overview

Our RCM module integrates directly with national payer gateways to provide real-time eligibility verification and claim status tracking. By leveraging predictive analytics, the system identifies potential coding errors (CPT/ICD-10) before submission, reducing denial rates by an average of 35% within the first quarter of implementation.

Core Capabilities

  • Automated Claim Scrubbing: Pre-submission algorithms analyzing claims key-value pairs against payer-specific rulesets.
  • Denial Management Workflow: Categorized work queues for rejected claims with root-cause analysis tooltips.
  • Patient Financial Experience: Digital wallet integration (Apple Pay, Google Pay) and automated payment plan structuring.
  • Ledger Reconciliation: Daily automated sync (EOD) with bank deposits and ERP systems.

Implementation Timeline

Week 1: Gateway Configuration. Secure API handshake with clearinghouses and merchant processors.
Week 2: Historical Migrations. Transfer of aging AR (Accounts Receivable) and patient ledgers.
Week 3: Rule Definition. Customizing fee schedules and denial routing logic.
Week 4: Live Transactional Stream. Full cutover to automated billing cycle.

Clinical Impact Study

Multi-Specialty Clinic, New York

Challenge: 45-day average reimbursement cycle and high manual entry error rate.
Outcome: Reduced DSO to 18 days and recaptured $140k in under-billed revenue annually.

Intelligent Scheduling & Patient Access

Maximize provider utilization and patient adherence through a heuristic scheduling engine and omnichannel engagement framework.

[Image Placeholder: A split-screen view. Left side: A doctor's dynamic calendar view with color-coded appointment types. Right side: A mobile interface showing a patient confirming a slot via WhatsApp.]

Technical Overview

The system utilizes a proprietary algorithm to balance provider preferences, appointment acuity, and room availability. Access controls allow for granular configuration of "self-scheduling" rules, ensuring that complex procedures are vetted while routine follow-ups remain frictionless for patients.

Core Capabilities

  • Dynamic Availability Engine: Real-time slot calculation based on procedure duration and provider templates.
  • Omnichannel Reminders: Cascading notification logic (SMS -> WhatsApp -> IVR Call) to confirm attendance 24hr/48hr prior.

  • Bi-Directional Calendar Sync: seamless integration with Outlook/Google Workspace for staff availability.

Implementation Timeline

Day 1: Configuration. Define provider schedules, appointment types, and durations.
Day 2: Patient Sync. Import patient contact list for automated notifications.
Day 3: Portal Launch. Activate online booking portal and send announcement SMS to patients.
Week 2: Optimization. Fine-tune reminder cadence and waitlist automation based on initial data.

Outcome: Reduced no-show rate from 18% to 4% within 60 days, adding 12 billable hours per week.

Clinical Decision Support & Ambient AI Scribe

Augment clinical expertise with an always-on neural network designed to alleviate documentation disputes and enhance diagnostic precision.

[Image Placeholder: An abstract visualization of a "Voice-to-Text" waveform transforming into a structured SOAP note on a tablet screen, with key medical terms highlighted.]

Technical Overview

Built on a specialized Large Language Model (LLM) tuned on millions of de-identified clinical encounters. The Ambient AI Scribe passively listens to patient-provider dialogue, filtering casual conversation to extract and capitalize distinct medical necessities, populating the EHR in real-time.

Core Capabilities

  • Ambient Documentation: Zero-click generation of HPI (History of Present Illness) and Physical Exam notes.
  • Real-time Interaction Checks: Alerting providers to potential contraindications based on current meds and new prescriptions.
  • Coding Assistance: Automated suggestion of E&M (Evaluation and Management) codes based on documentation complexity.

Implementation Timeline

Day 1: Plugin Installation. Deploy ambient listening hardware and integrate with EMR.
Day 3: Voice Calibration. AI learns provider-specific accent, terminology, and speech patterns.
Day 7: Accuracy Review. First weekly audit to fine-tune AI suggestions and reduce false positives.
Day 14: Full Deployment. Live documentation with real-time clinical decision support.

Clinical Impact Study

Internal Medicine Practice, Chicago

Challenge: Providers spending 2+ hours on documentation after clinic hours.
Outcome: Reduced charting time by 70%, allowing physicians to see 3 additional patients daily.

Electronic Medical Records (EMR/EHR)

A longitudinal, interoperable patient data repository designed for high-velocity retrieval and secure interoperability (FHIR/HL7).

[Image Placeholder: A "Patient 360" view interface. Central focus on a patient avatar surrounded by interactive widgets: Recent Labs, Vitals Trend Graph, and Chronic Conditions List.]

Technical Overview

Our cloud-native architecture ensures sub-second query response times for patient histories, regardless of dataset size. Security protocols include AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit, fully compliant with HIPAA, GDPR, and SOC2 Type II standards.

Core Capabilities

  • Interoperability: Seamless HL7 data exchange with external labs (Quest, LabCorp) and hospital systems.
  • Role-Based Access Control (RBAC): Granular permissions ensuring staff strictly access "Need-to-Know" PHI.
  • Integrated Vitals Tracking: Direct IoT ingestion from connected blood pressure cuffs and scales.

Implementation Timeline

Week 1: Chart Digitization. Professional scanning team digitizes active paper charts.
Week 2: Cloud Migration. Encrypted transfer of digital files to HIPAA-compliant cloud storage.
Week 3: Data Verification. Providers verify data integrity before archiving physical records.
Week 4: Go Live. Full EMR system launch with staff training and support.

Clinical Impact Study

Family Practice, Seattle

Challenge: 15,000+ paper charts taking up valuable office space and slow retrieval times.
Outcome: 100% digital conversion in 3 weeks, reducing chart retrieval time from 5 minutes to 5 seconds.

Core Capabilities
